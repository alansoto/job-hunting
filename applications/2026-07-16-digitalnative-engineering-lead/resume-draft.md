## Experience

### Xero - Lead Technical Program Manager
Mar 2022 - Jul 2026 · Brisbane, QLD

Global cloud accounting platform, 4,500+ employees, products used by over 3 million customers. I led company-wide engineering programs spanning dozens of teams, reporting to GM and EGM-level leadership.

Most of my work here was technical direction and engineering practice rather than schedule management. I spent my time on architecture decisions, migration sequencing, and making other engineering leaders better at running their teams.

**Challenge & Impact**
- **Problem:** Xero's product capabilities were scattered across a sprawl of ageing, inconsistent APIs. Opening them up to AI agents and third parties meant moving every engineering division onto a single federated API, a migration touching virtually every team in the company. Nothing had been proven at production scale, no team owned the path off the legacy systems, and every domain had a good reason to let someone else go first.
- **Solution:** Instead of running it as a big-bang program, I found the smallest slice of real functionality that could prove the platform and took it all the way to production, keeping it deliberately narrow so it would not drag the legacy system's brittleness across with it. It went live as the first set of features served by the new API, for a real customer-facing team. I then used a second, high-traffic team as the scale test, de-risking their cutover by starting at 1% of live traffic. With the platform proven twice over, I built the single company-wide view of every division's migration and retirement plan, which became the agreed roadmap and schedule the whole program ran to.

**Key Contributions**
- Coached engineering managers and tech leads across 10+ teams on cycle time, capacity planning, forecasting and healthy delivery rhythms, improving flow and predictability without adding process.
- Embedded in Code Red, a division-wide CI/CD capability uplift. Implemented observability tooling for the four DORA metrics and personally built the automated Lead Time for Changes dashboards, then facilitated the team sessions that established SLOs, on-call practices and progressive delivery.
- Launched a Xero-wide Jira optimisation initiative and a division-wide Jira Data Quality Score, turning a local tooling problem into an organisational capability and cutting planning and reporting toil at scale.
- Designed cross-functional ways of working for the Quokkas team: separated roadmap tracking from execution tracking, made Confluence the discovery home, and set explicit norms for communication and feedback loops.
