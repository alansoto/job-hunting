The Opportunity

An opportunity exists for an experienced ICT Project Manager to lead the delivery of a strategic fleet technology transformation project. This 12-month contract will establish the foundations for a new enterprise technology capability by leading project governance, business requirements, market engagement, pilot delivery and implementation planning. Working closely with ICT, operational, procurement and business stakeholders, you will play a pivotal role in delivering a solution that enhances fleet visibility, safety, compliance and operational performance across a large and diverse fleet.

The Role

In this role, you will lead the successful establishment and delivery of the project by:

    Establishing project governance, delivery controls, reporting, schedules and stakeholder engagement frameworks.
    Coordinating business requirements across operational, ICT and corporate stakeholders to define technology capability and business needs.
    Managing market engagement activities including RFIs, vendor evaluations, procurement planning and solution assessments.
    Leading planning and coordination of a Proof of Concept and pilot, including readiness, installation, communications, training and support.
    Managing project risks, issues, budgets, dependencies, schedules, governance and benefits realisation throughout delivery.
    Providing clear reporting and decision-ready recommendations to senior stakeholders and project governance groups.

About You

To succeed in this role, you will bring proven experience delivering ICT-enabled business transformation projects within complex operational environments.

    Demonstrated experience managing medium-scale ICT projects from establishment through to implementation.
    Strong project management capability across scope, schedule, governance, budget, risk and stakeholder management.
    Experience leading business requirements gathering, solution evaluation, procurement activities and vendor engagement.
    Ability to engage effectively with operational teams, ICT specialists, executives, procurement and external suppliers.
    Strong communication skills with the ability to translate business needs into practical delivery outcomes.
    Experience working within large, complex or government environments will be highly regarded.
