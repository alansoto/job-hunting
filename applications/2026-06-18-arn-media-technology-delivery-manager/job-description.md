# Technology Delivery Manager — ARN Media

Date: 2 Jun 2026
Location: Milton, QLD, AU, 4064
Company: ARN Media

At ARN, we’re building Australia’s leading entertainment company — where great content, powerful audiences and innovative platforms come together to drive growth.

From our iconic broadcast brands to podcasts, streaming, social and live experiences, we create content that entertains Australians and connects brands to audiences at scale. With a national footprint across metro and regional markets and a clear focus on digital acceleration, audience growth and commercial impact, ARN is evolving fast — and our people play a critical role in making it happen.

We’re actively looking for a Technology Delivery Manager to play a critical role in translating strategy into execution and ensuring we deliver with pace, quality and confidence. Sitting within the Technology team and reporting to the Head of Technology, you’ll be responsible for ensuring our technology and data initiatives are delivered reliably, predictably and aligned to business priorities. You’ll work across business-critical enterprise systems and high-availability broadcast, content and digital platforms, supporting outcomes that matter to both our audiences and our commercial performance.

## About the role:

- **End-to-end delivery ownership:** Lead delivery of data and development initiatives, ensuring outcomes are delivered to agreed.
- **Planning & coordination:** Build and maintain integrated delivery plans, managing dependencies, sequencing and resourcing across multiple teams.
- **Governance & cadence:** Facilitate planning, prioritisation and delivery forums, including stand-ups, showcases and governance updates. Provide clear, transparent reporting on progress, risks and outcomes
- **Risk & issue management:** Proactively identify and manage risks, dependencies and trade-offs, ensuring mitigation actions are in place and tracked
- **Stakeholder alignment:** Partner closely with Product Owners, Data and Technology stakeholders to ensure priorities are clear and delivery remains outcome focused.
- **Offshore & vendor management:** Coordinate across onshore and offshore teams, ensuring clear requirements, strong cadence and aligned expectations. Drive vendor performance through clear scopes, measurable deliverables and quality standards.
- **Quality & release readiness:** Ensure delivery quality through testing, data validation and release readiness, balancing speed with stability and governance.
- **Continuous improvement:** Strengthen delivery ways of working, tools and practices to improve predictability, quality and efficiency across the program.

## About you:

- Proven experience leading delivery across data and/or software development initiatives.
- Strong planning and dependency management capability across multiple workstreams.
- Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills — confident influencing and aligning across teams.
- Experience coordinating onshore and offshore delivery teams.
- Strong vendor management capability, including performance monitoring and issue resolution.
- Solid understanding of delivery practices (Agile or hybrid), governance and reporting.
- Working knowledge of data and analytics delivery (e.g. pipelines, BI/reporting, APIs) and the broader software development lifecycle.
