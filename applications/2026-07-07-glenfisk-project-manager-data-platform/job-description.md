## About the role

We are seeking two experienced Project Managers x2 to join a major transformation program. This is an excellent opportunity to lead the successful delivery of strategic projects within a large enterprise environment.

You will work closely with business stakeholders, technical teams and governance groups to manage project delivery, ensuring outcomes are achieved on time, within budget and aligned with broader program objectives.

### Project: CRM Proof of Concept / Client Portal (including identity management)

This initiative will validate and identify the preferred Customer Relationship Management (CRM) solution through a Proof of Concept, and then deliver a client-facing portal incorporating the CRM solution, identity verification and access management capabilities, workflow automation and case management functionality.

### Project: Data Platform and Integration

This initiative will establish a modern enterprise data and integration platform, leveraging Databricks and cross-agency data sources, to provide secure, governed and scalable access to fines information. The solution will support data ingestion, integration, reporting, analytics and information sharing across the organisation and partner agencies, providing a foundational capability for future digital services, customer channels, operational decision-making and modernised fines administration.
