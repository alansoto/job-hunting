## Project decision

This ad doesn't explicitly ask candidates to nominate a project (no "Stream 1/2" language or employer
question), but the two initiatives are identical to Karlka's. Consistent with the Karlka application,
**targeting Data Platform and Integration** — Suncorp's data-platform/telephony migration and Xero's
API/integration program are the strongest matches; there's no CRM, case-management, identity-verification
or client-portal evidence in the wiki for the other project.

## Strong matches

- Complex ICT/technology delivery across business, technology, architecture, security, vendor
  stakeholders — Xero (API Program/Supergraph) and Suncorp (telephony/data-platform migration,
  Infrastructure/Security/Business units/external vendors). → [[experience]]
- Project governance, schedules, budgets, risks, issues, dependencies — Unified Burndown (Xero),
  delivery governance frameworks (Suncorp). → [[achievements]], [[keywords]]
- Executive briefings and stakeholder engagement up to EGM/GM/board level. → [[experience]], [[skills]]

## Gaps

- **Databricks** — no direct evidence; bridge via delivery-management framing (as in the Karlka cover
  letter) rather than claiming hands-on platform experience.
- **Complex government environment / cross-agency data sharing** — no direct government experience;
  Suncorp (ASX-listed, regulated financial services) is the standing analogue.

## Fit score: Moderate — same as Karlka application. Core PM/governance fundamentals are a strong match;
Databricks and government/cross-agency context remain the stretch areas, acknowledged plainly in the
cover letter.
