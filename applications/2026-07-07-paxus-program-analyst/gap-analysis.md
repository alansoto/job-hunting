# Gap Analysis — Program Analyst (Paxus)

## Positioning note (read first)

This is a **support/analyst role inside a PMO/Delivery Office** — reporting to Program and Project
Managers, not leading programs end-to-end. It sits well below Alan's usual target altitude (Lead
TPM / Delivery Lead / Principal PM, EGM-level stakeholder visibility, dozens of teams). The
capability match is strong, but the *framing* risk is overqualification, not underqualification.

Approach: lead with the hands-on analytical/reporting/tooling work (dashboards, Jira Data Quality
Score, SQL-backed reporting, RAID/dependency artefacts) rather than the "led dozens of teams,
EGM-level" scale claims. Downplay headline seniority — use a plain "Program Analyst" or "Project
Analyst" headline, not "Lead"/"Head of"/"Principal". Frame the Xero/Suncorp material as "built and
ran the reporting and schedule artefacts a Delivery Office runs on" rather than "owned the program."
This is a contract role, which supports a natural narrative for taking a slightly more execution-
focused position between/alongside larger PM engagements.
