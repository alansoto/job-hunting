About the Role

We're looking for an experienced Technical Program Manager to drive the successful delivery of our enterprise AI platform and customer implementations. This is a highly visible role that sits at the centre of engineering, architecture, deployment, and product delivery. You'll coordinate multiple technical workstreams, remove delivery blockers, manage dependencies, and ensure our engineering teams consistently deliver high-quality outcomes.

You'll thrive if you're someone who enjoys bringing structure to complex environments, aligning technical teams around common goals, and delivering mission-critical programs in a fast-moving, high-growth company.

What You'll be doing:

Leading end-to-end delivery of multiple concurrent technical programs and strategic initiatives.
Coordinating engineering, architecture, deployment, and platform teams to deliver enterprise AI solutions.
Driving release planning, production deployments, and go-live activities across multiple teams.
Managing delivery schedules, risks, dependencies, and technical blockers to keep programs on track.
Facilitating Agile ceremonies including sprint planning, stand-ups, retrospectives, and roadmap reviews.
Working closely with engineering leaders, architects, product managers, and executive stakeholders to align priorities.
Establishing effective delivery governance, reporting, and operating rhythms across the organisation.
Translating business objectives into executable technical delivery plans.
Continuously improving delivery processes, execution quality, and organisational efficiency.

What You'll bring:

Experience establishing delivery governance and program operating rhythms.
Experience translating business strategy into technical execution.
Background delivering developer platforms, APIs, AI platforms, or engineering enablement programs.
6+ years' experience in Technical Program Management, Technical Project Management, Delivery Leadership, or a similar role.
Proven experience delivering complex technical programs from planning through production release.
Demonstrated success managing multiple concurrent projects and competing priorities.
Strong experience coordinating cross-functional engineering, architecture, and deployment teams.
Experience leading enterprise software releases and production deployments.
Strong Agile and Scrum delivery experience.
