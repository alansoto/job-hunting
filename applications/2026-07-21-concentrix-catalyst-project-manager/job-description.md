About the job

Concentrix Catalyst is looking for a forward-thinking, Project Manager with exceptional communication skills and experience in Agile, Scrum and digital technology projects to join a team that thrives on solving complex challenges.


About Us:

Concentrix Catalyst, a global digital services company, is at the forefront of strategic consulting, service and experience design, multi-platform engineering, and systems integration. As a key player within the Concentrix family since 2017, we've rebranded to Concentrix Catalyst to better reflect our evolving services and seamless integration into Concentrix's strategic offerings.


Your Role: Project Manager

As a Project Manager, you will be a strategic leader, responsible for guiding our clients through complex digital evolution. You will not only deliver projects but also act as a trusted advisor, shaping their digital future and ensuring our solutions drive tangible business value.

    Lead larger, complex projects and manage multiple concurrent projects for the same client with a focus on enabling their digital transformation.
    Provide governance, stability, and proactive improvements within the Project Management Team.
    Build and maintain strong client relationships, serving as the single point of contact.
    Lead and inspire diverse teams of UX/UI experts, software engineers, and analysts.
    Drive continuous improvement and innovation in project delivery.
    Partner with clients to analyse their business objectives and translate them into actionable digital roadmaps and plans.


What You Bring:

    Minimum 5+ years of Project Management experience in a digital environment.
    Expertise in digital/software delivery projects, particularly in the Native Mobile space.
    Strong grasp of Agile methodologies (Kanban) with hands-on experience in technical and digital projects.
    Proven success in managing diverse teams, including local and global offices, and remote workers.
    Experience with Google Suite, Confluence, JIRA, Smartsheet, and other project tools.


Perks and Benefits:

    Flexible hybrid work environment .
    Competitive holiday allowance, increasing with tenure.
    End-of-year performance bonus
    Free lunch Thursdays.
