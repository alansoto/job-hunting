## Fit score

**Strong — 8/10.**

Every core delivery requirement is directly evidenced. The SaaS-to-market track record is genuine and deep across multiple industries. The regulated-industry credential (Suncorp) is solid even though it is not healthcare. The on-prem-to-cloud gap is real but the API Program provides a credible framing. The healthcare domain is new but the JD's own language ("an exciting start to your Health IT career") signals openness to non-healthcare candidates. The Masters of PM (Dean's Award) and PMP training are a clean credential match. The biggest risk is the hiring panel placing heavy weight on clinical/TGA experience — which is not present. Best framing: strong delivery generalist who has consistently succeeded in regulated, complex, cross-functional environments.
