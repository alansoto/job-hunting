Are you ready to be a big part of something big?

A career in Software Engineering will see you working on products that are used every single day by our millions of customers all over the world. We embrace hybrid working combining the flexibility of remote work with the energy and connection of in-person collaboration. We champion flexibility and offer a range of policies and leave options to support your wellbeing.

## What you'll be doing

- Own engineering execution for Machines4U, from product development and platform reliability to engineering practices and delivery cadence
- Translate business priorities set by senior leadership into a clear, sequenced engineering roadmap and deliver against it
- Lead a high-performing, distributed engineering team, and hold the behavioural and technical bar
- Partner closely with product and carsales engineering counterparts to shape the Machines4U technology landscape and ensure engineering decisions serve commercial outcomes
- Lead the progressive modernisation of Machines4U's technology stack, balancing legacy platform stability with migration to scalable, secure and group-aligned modern systems.
- Oversee key platform integrations and third-party technical dependencies, ensuring reliable interfaces across marketplace, CRM, payments, marketing, reporting and group systems.
- Own engineering input into security, reliability and compliance practices, ensuring risks are understood, prioritised and remediated pragmatically.
- Actively contribute to engineering practices, tooling, and culture across the carsales Engineering community
- Provide clear, credible engineering perspectives to senior leadership, including assessments of risk, trade-offs, and capacity

## What we're looking for

- Proven experience leading engineering teams in a product-led, commercially-driven environment, ideally in a marketplace or B2B SaaS context
- Strong technical depth across modern web stacks (backend and frontend), cloud infrastructure, and API design
- Track record delivering practical engineering roadmaps that move the needle on business outcomes
- Experience operating in a mixed legacy and modern technology environment, with a pragmatic approach to modernisation and risk reduction.
- Natural relationship builder who can work effectively across functions, holding your own with stakeholders while staying accountable to a broader group
- Credible operating range: from coding standards and incident response to roadmap trade-offs and senior leadership updates
- Hands-on enough to review architecture, unblock engineers, and spot quality problems early
- Pragmatic experience balancing delivery pace and meeting standards for security, reliability, and scalability
