About the Role
We're looking for a hands-on Senior Delivery Lead to drive the execution of product initiatives and customer outcomes within our teams. This is a highly embedded role where you'll partner closely with Product, Engineering, and Design to ensure work is delivered efficiently, predictably, and to a high standard.

You won't just facilitate you'll take ownership of execution, optimise team performance, and navigate risks, dependencies, and trade-offs to ensure value reaches customers.

Key Responsibilities
Own end-to-end execution of initiatives, from planning through to release and benefits realisation

Create clear, realistic plans, proactively tracking progress and surfacing risks early

Optimise team flow, pace, and quality through insight-led improvements

Lead agile ceremonies with a focus on outcomes, not process

Identify and remove blockers while managing cross-team dependencies

Enable incremental delivery of meaningful customer outcomes

Manage incidents and defects, ensuring root causes are addressed

Provide clear, actionable insights on progress, risks, and performance

Ensure alignment with broader business and portfolio priorities

About You
You're a pragmatic, hands-on Senior Delivery Lead who thrives in product-led environments and knows how to get things done. You balance pace with quality and technical health, staying proactive in identifying challenges and driving outcomes.

A confident collaborator, you build alignment across Product, Engineering, and stakeholders, using data and insights to strengthen team performance and delivery predictability.

You'll bring
Experience in delivery roles within modern product and engineering teams

Strong understanding of the software delivery lifecycle and engineering practices (TDD, BDD, etc.)

DevOps experience (CI/CD, observability, infrastructure as code)

Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)

Strong Atlassian expertise (Jira, JPD, Compass, Analytics)

Familiarity with GitHub workflows and release pipelines

Excellent communication, problem-solving, and stakeholder engagement skills

A continuous improvement mindset with the ability to uplift team performance
