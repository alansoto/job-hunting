## Your New Team

The Core Platform Services Delivery team operates within Auto & General's Technology division, concentrating on agile delivery of technology solutions that support insurance products and operations. The team emphasizes collaborative work, seamless execution, continuous improvement, and enabling rapid business movement without sacrificing quality standards.

## The Role

As a Delivery Lead, you will serve as a key contributor to technology delivery, championing agile practices, coordinating cross-functional teams, and ensuring timely, high-quality solutions aligned with business objectives. This hands-on individual contributor position leverages your influence, leadership, and delivery expertise to shape team outcomes.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Lead end-to-end agile delivery of technology projects, ensuring alignment with business objectives, timelines, and quality standards
- Facilitate agile ceremonies including sprint planning, daily stand-ups, sprint reviews, and retrospectives
- Empower self-organising teams by removing impediments and fostering a high-performance, collaborative culture
- Engage with stakeholders across business units, IT teams, and external partners to understand priorities and manage expectations
- Identify, assess, and mitigate delivery risks and dependencies, escalating where appropriate
- Define, track, and report on delivery metrics and KPIs to senior management and stakeholders
- Drive continuous improvement initiatives using feedback loops, retrospectives, and data-driven insights
- Support APRA compliance obligations, contributing to risk management frameworks, policies, and reporting
- Assist with APRA audits and examinations, operating within A&G's Code of Conduct at all times

## What Experience You'll Bring

- Proven experience as a Delivery Lead within an agile environment
- Experience in the insurance or financial services industry (highly regarded)
- Strong working knowledge of agile methodologies including Scrum and Kanban
- Demonstrated ability to lead agile transformation initiatives and embed agile ways of working
- Solid understanding of the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC), DevOps practices, and release management
- Excellent stakeholder management, communication, and leadership skills with the ability to influence across all levels
- Experience working in APRA-regulated environments or similar compliance-focused settings (advantageous)
- Relevant certifications such as PMP, CSM, or equivalent (preferred)
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Administration, or a related field
