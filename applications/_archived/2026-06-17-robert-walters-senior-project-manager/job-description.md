## Job Description

This role involves leading a major digital transformation initiative. The successful candidate will oversee a website redevelopment project as part of a broader program, reporting directly to the Program Manager.

## Key Responsibilities

- Deliver website redevelopment within a larger program framework
- Manage Optimizely platform implementation, including integrations and data migration
- Coordinate cross-functional teams, external vendors, and delivery partners
- Manage timelines, budgets, RAID documentation, and governance structures
- Engage stakeholders across business and technical divisions
- Oversee testing, deployment, and transition phases

## Required Qualifications

- Demonstrated experience delivering website or digital platform projects within programs
- CMS platform knowledge, particularly Optimizely
- Experience in structured program environments reporting to senior leadership
- Strong vendor management and stakeholder engagement abilities
- Understanding of integrations, APIs, and data migration
- Excellent organizational and delivery-focused approach
- Experience across both public and private sector organizations preferred
