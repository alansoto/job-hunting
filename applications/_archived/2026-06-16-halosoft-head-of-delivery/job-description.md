## About Halosoft

Halosoft develops AI-powered software for the financial advice industry. Their products — Meeting Ally, Advice Ally, and Odin — help advice businesses reduce rework, improve consistency, automate advice workflows, and scale their operations.

---

## Role Summary

A hands-on leadership position focused on establishing delivery structure within a fast-moving SaaS environment. The Head of Delivery will own the delivery operating rhythm across multiple product streams, including web, mobile, AI automation, integrations, QA, and release management.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Lead delivery cadence across engineering, design, QA, and product teams
- Transform product priorities into clear delivery plans and milestones
- Enhance sprint planning and backlog readiness
- Track committed versus delivered work
- Identify blockers and delivery risks early
- Maintain accurate Jira documentation
- Coordinate QA, testing, and production validation
- Provide leadership visibility on delivery progress
- Support continuous improvement through retrospectives

---

## Required Qualifications

- Delivery, project management, or engineering delivery experience
- SaaS or software product background
- Strong Jira and agile expertise
- Remote team coordination experience
- Software delivery and QA understanding

## Preferred Experience

- Fintech or financial services background
- AI-enabled product experience
- Mobile app delivery knowledge
- Integration and API familiarity
- Scaling startup environment
