## About the Role

This hybrid position sits at the intersection of product and delivery, requiring someone to own outcomes, shape priorities, and drive delivery through a complex environment. The successful candidate will operate across squads or initiatives, bridging product definition with execution.

## Key Responsibilities

- Convert business objectives into clear outcomes and actionable backlogs
- Lead comprehensive delivery while managing dependencies and obstacles
- Align stakeholders from both business and technology sectors on deliverables
- Make balanced decisions considering value, risk, and technical feasibility
- Sustain momentum amid shifting priorities
- Contribute to initiatives spanning digital, CRM, and customer/adviser platforms

## Required Qualifications

- Experience in Agile or iterative delivery environments
- Background as a Product Owner, Delivery Lead, or comparable role
- Strong cross-functional stakeholder management abilities
- Capacity to balance competing demands under delivery pressure
- Pragmatic, action-oriented approach focused on outcomes
- Familiarity with digital platforms, CRM, or CIAM systems
- Knowledge of APIs, integration work, or cloud infrastructure (AWS/Azure)
