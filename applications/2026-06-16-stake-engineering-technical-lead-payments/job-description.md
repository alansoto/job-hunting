## About the role

The Engineering Technical Lead - Payments will lead engineering teams building and scaling Stake's payment capabilities — from payment processing and reconciliation through to banking integrations, fraud controls and operational tooling.

This is a role for someone who can balance people leadership with technical decision-making, and who understands that trust, reliability and operational excellence are what matter most in payments.

### What you'll do

- Lead one or more engineering squads focused on payments
- Own and evolve the technical direction and architectural standards for the payments domain
- Partner closely with Product, Operations, Finance, Risk and Compliance teams
- Improve platform reliability, scalability and operational resilience
- Establish and drive engineering best practices across the team

### What you'll bring

- Experience leading engineering teams with a genuine balance of people leadership and technical decision-making
- Strong focus on customer outcomes and a deep understanding of trust, reliability and operational excellence in payments
- Sound technical judgement to make good architecture and platform decisions
- Ability to thrive in fast-moving environments with complex, ambiguous problems
- Track record of cross-functional stakeholder alignment and communication

### Nice to have

- Experience in payments, banking, fintech or financial product development
- Familiarity with payment processors, banking integrations or reconciliation systems
- Prior leadership of high-availability, mission-critical systems
- Experience collaborating with Risk, Compliance, Fraud or Operations teams
- Third-party integrations and vendor management
- Experience scaling engineering organisations
