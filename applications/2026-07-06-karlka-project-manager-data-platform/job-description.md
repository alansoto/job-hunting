## About the company
Karlka Recruiting Group has partnered with a State Government Agency to engage two experienced Project Managers to support Stage 2 of a major Fine Modernisation Program.

This position is located in Brisbane City, QLD for a 12-month contract with possible extensions.

There are two separate project streams available under the same Project Manager title:

1. CRM Proof of Concept and Client Portal, including identity management
2. Data Platform and Integration, leveraging Databricks and cross-agency data sources

Applicants should clearly identify which stream best aligns with their experience. Candidates with relevant experience across both streams may nominate both.

## About the Position
The Project Managers will lead the planning, governance and delivery of complex ICT initiatives supporting the modernisation of Queensland's fines and penalty debt systems.

Each Project Manager will be responsible for one project stream and will work across business, technology, architecture, security, data, vendor and external agency stakeholders.

The roles will ensure projects are delivered in accordance with agreed scope, budget, schedule, quality and governance requirements, while aligning with the broader objectives of the Fine Modernisation Program.

### Stream One – CRM Proof of Concept and Client Portal
This project will validate and identify the preferred Customer Relationship Management solution through a Proof of Concept before progressing delivery of a client-facing portal.

The solution will incorporate CRM functionality, identity verification and access management, workflow automation and case management capabilities.

### Stream Two – Data Platform and Integration
This project will establish a modern enterprise data and integration platform leveraging Databricks and cross-agency data sources.

The platform will provide secure, governed and scalable access to fines information and support data ingestion, integration, reporting, analytics and information sharing.
