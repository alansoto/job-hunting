## The Opportunity

This role involves partnering with Queensland Government to fill a senior leadership position focused on delivering high-profile ICT and business transformation initiatives. The successful candidate will manage end-to-end project delivery and influence senior stakeholders while shaping governance structures.

## Key Responsibilities

- Lead complex ICT and business change programs
- Establish project governance, reporting, and methodology standards
- Manage scope, budget, risks, and delivery timelines
- Engage and advise senior stakeholders and project boards
- Lead and mentor project teams
- Provide high-quality reporting on milestones and outcomes
- Ensure projects are delivered on time, on budget and to agreed outcomes

## Requirements

- Proven experience with large, complex ICT or transformation programs
- Government or enterprise governance background
- Strong stakeholder engagement and influencing capabilities
- Demonstrated team leadership and development ability
- Financial, risk, and delivery management skills
- Executive and board-level communication experience
- PRINCE2, Agile, or similar certifications preferred

## Why Apply

- Opportunity to shape digital government services across Queensland
- Leadership role with influence beyond delivery
- Collaborative, purpose-driven environment
- Brisbane CBD location
